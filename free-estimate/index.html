Choosing new floors should start with real numbers, not a guess. Whether you'd rather have us come measure your rooms or you'd like to bring your dimensions into the Carson City showroom, a free flooring estimate from Landmark gives you an accurate, itemized picture of what your project will actually cost — material, labor, removal and all.

There's never a fee for an estimate and never an expectation that you'll sign anything that day. We'd rather you take the number home, sleep on it, and come back when the floor and the price both feel right.

What your free estimate includes

An in-home flooring consultation with Landmark is hands-on and specific to your space — not a phone quote based on averages. Here's what you can expect when we visit:

  • Accurate measuring: we measure every room, closet, stairway and transition so the square-footage on your quote is real, not rounded up.
  • Honest material advice: we'll talk through what holds up best for your household and our dry, high-desert climate — waterproof luxury vinyl for pets and kids, engineered hardwood that resists gapping, soft carpet for bedrooms, or budget-friendly laminate.
  • Subfloor & prep check: we look for moisture, flatness and old-floor removal needs up front, so there are no surprise add-ons later.
  • A clear written quote: one number covering material, our warrantied installation, removal and haul-away — no vague ranges.
  • Financing options: if you'd like to spread the cost, we'll explain the financing available so you can install now and pay over time.

Prefer to start in person? Visit our showroom — see the full range of services we install, feel real samples, and preview your favorites with the in-store visualizer before we ever measure.
