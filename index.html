Questions

Flooring Questions, Answered

Do you offer free flooring estimates?
Yes. Every project starts with a free, no-pressure estimate. Visit our Carson City showroom or call (775) 297-3236 and we'll help you choose the right floor for your space and budget, then measure and quote at no charge.
What areas do you serve?
We serve all of Northern Nevada from our Carson City showroom — including Reno, Sparks, Minden, Gardnerville, Lake Tahoe, Virginia City and Fallon — for residential, property-management and commercial flooring.
Should I choose luxury vinyl (LVP), laminate, or hardwood?
It depends on your rooms, budget and lifestyle. Waterproof luxury vinyl (LVP) is the most durable and pet- and kid-friendly choice; laminate offers a wood look for less; hardwood adds lasting, timeless value. We'll walk you through the trade-offs in our showroom.
Is waterproof flooring a good choice for pets and kids?
Yes. Waterproof luxury vinyl (LVP/LVT) stands up to spills, muddy paws and busy households, and its tough wear layer resists scratches and dents — making it one of the best floors for homes with pets and kids in Northern Nevada's dry, high-desert climate.
Do you offer financing?
Yes, financing is available so you can get the floor you want now and pay over time. Ask us about current financing options when you visit or call for your free estimate.
Are you licensed and insured, and is the work guaranteed?
Yes. Landmark Flooring is licensed, bonded and insured, and every job is backed by The Landmark Guarantee — warrantied products plus our own warrantied, in-house labor, so you're covered on both the floor and the installation.
