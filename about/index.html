The Landmark Guarantee

The Landmark Guarantee is the promise the whole business is built on, and it has two halves that most flooring outfits split apart. First, warrantied products: we sell floors backed by their manufacturers' warranties, and we help you actually understand what those cover. Second — and this is the part big-box stores can't match — warrantied labor. The skilled installers on our team do the installation, and we stand behind that workmanship. Because the same company is responsible for the material and the install, you never get caught in the middle of a store blaming the installer and the installer blaming the store. One number to call, one team accountable.

Skilled installers on our team, real craftsmanship

The single biggest variable in how a floor turns out isn't the brand on the box — it's who installs it. That's why every floor is put in by the skilled installers on our team. Carpet that's power-stretched and seamed correctly lies flat and lasts for years; carpet that's kicked into place ripples. Hardwood that's properly acclimated and fastened stays tight; rushed wood gaps and cups. Luxury vinyl over a flat, prepped subfloor performs like it should; vinyl over an uneven floor telegraphs every dip. Our installers sweat those details because their name — and ours — is on the result. See exactly how a project runs, from measure to haul-away, on our installation page.

Local climate expertise you can't get nationally

Northern Nevada is a high-desert climate, and floors behave differently here than they do in a humid coastal market. Our dry air and big day-to-night temperature swings pull moisture out of solid wood, which is why we so often steer Carson City and Reno homeowners toward engineered hardwood or rigid-core luxury vinyl, and why we take acclimation seriously on every wood job. Up at Tahoe, the math flips — snowmelt and seasonal moisture make waterproof floors and careful transitions matter more. Knowing which floor survives which Northern Nevada microclimate is exactly the kind of experience a national website can't offer you. If you want to dig in, our guide to the right install for our climate walks through acclimation and why it prevents gapping.
