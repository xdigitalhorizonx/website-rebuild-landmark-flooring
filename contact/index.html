What to expect in the showroom

A flooring decision is much easier to make in person than on a screen. At our Carson City showroom you can run your hand across real samples, compare wear layers and pile heights side by side, and see how a wood-look plank or a plush carpet actually reads in natural light. There's no pressure and no appointment required — come browse, ask questions, and leave with samples to live with at home for a few days.

Our team knows Northern Nevada homes. We'll talk through how our dry, high-desert climate affects each material — why engineered hardwood and rigid-core luxury vinyl tend to stay flatter through Carson City's big temperature swings than solid wood does — and steer you toward the floor that fits your rooms, your household and your budget rather than the most expensive option on the rack.

In-store visualizer & samples

Use our in-store visualizer to preview your top picks in a room before you commit, so you can compare colors and plank widths without guessing. Take physical samples home to check them against your cabinets, trim and lighting at different times of day — the same oak can look golden in the showroom and cooler in a north-facing bedroom. When you've narrowed it down, we'll measure, advise and quote your project at no charge.

How to prepare for your visit

You don't need anything to walk in and browse, but a little prep helps us give you accurate guidance and a faster path to an estimate:

  • Rough room dimensions (length × width) for each space you're flooring
  • A few photos of the rooms and your current flooring or subfloor
  • A ballpark budget and which rooms are the priority
  • Notes on how each room is used — pets, kids, moisture, heavy traffic
  • Any inspiration photos of looks you love
