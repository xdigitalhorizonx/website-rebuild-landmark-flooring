How we cover the region from one Carson City showroom

Because Carson City sits at the center of the region, most of our service area is a short drive away. Reno and Sparks are roughly 30 to 40 minutes up US-395, Minden and Gardnerville are 15 to 20 minutes south through the Carson Valley, and the Tahoe-area communities are a scenic climb into the mountains. That central spot is exactly why a single, well-run local team can handle the whole region without losing the personal, accountable service you'd expect from a neighborhood store.

What working with us looks like

For homeowners outside Carson City, the process is simple: you reach out, we schedule a free in-home visit, and one of our team members brings samples to your door, measures your rooms, and talks through options that suit Northern Nevada's dry, high-desert climate — where the right material (think waterproof luxury vinyl or properly acclimated engineered hardwood) makes a real long-term difference. You get a written, no-pressure estimate, and if you move forward, the same company that quoted the job installs it.

  • We travel to you. Free in-home measure and quote across the service area — no charge, no obligation.
  • One accountable team. We sell the floor and install it — we use skilled installers and warranty our labor, backed by The Landmark Guarantee.
  • Climate-smart advice. Recommendations tuned to high-desert dryness, big day-to-night temperature swings, and Tahoe moisture.
  • Licensed, bonded & insured.
