At Landmark Flooring we believe a budget shouldn't force you to settle for a floor you don't love. That's why we offer flooring financing to homeowners across Carson City and Northern Nevada — a simple way to spread the cost of materials and professional installation into predictable monthly payments instead of one large up-front bill. Whether you're refreshing a single room or putting new floors throughout the house, financing keeps the project moving on your timeline.

How flooring financing works & how to apply

The process is designed to be quick and low-stress, and it fits naturally into the way we already work with customers:

  1. Start with a free estimate. Visit our Carson City showroom or have us measure your space at no charge, so you know the real scope and an accurate number before you finance anything.
  2. Choose your floor. Pick the material and style you want from our premium and budget-friendly lines — financing covers both.
  3. Apply. Ask us about financing and we'll walk you through a short application. Approval decisions are typically fast, so you usually know where you stand the same day.
  4. Install now, pay over time. Once you're approved, we schedule your installation and you pay for the project in monthly payments instead of all at once.

Financing is subject to credit approval, and the available plans, terms and rates are set by our financing partner. Ask our team for the current details that apply to your project — we'll lay out the real options in plain language before you commit to anything.

What flooring financing can cover

Because Landmark both supplies and installs every floor we sell, financing can be applied to the entire project — not just the boxes of flooring. That typically includes:

  • Materials across every category — carpet, luxury vinyl, laminate, hardwood and custom rugs.
  • Professional installation with warrantied labor from the skilled installers on our team.
  • Prep work like old-floor removal, haul-away, subfloor leveling and trim — the parts of a quote that surprise people most.

Rolling the whole job into one financed amount means you deal with one local team and one monthly payment, instead of juggling separate costs for product and labor.

Budget and premium options — both finance the same way

One of the best things about financing is that it lets you weigh your choices on what's right for your home rather than purely on the up-front price. A more durable, higher-end floor often costs only a little more per month once it's spread out, while still delivering years of extra wear. We carry quality options at every tier, so you can compare honestly:

  • Waterproof, pet- and kid-friendly value: our luxury vinyl flooring is the floor we most often recommend for busy Northern Nevada homes and rentals.
  • Timeless warmth and resale value: hardwood flooring — including engineered options well-suited to our dry high-desert climate — is a premium upgrade financing makes far more attainable.

Not sure which direction fits your budget and your rooms? That's exactly what our free estimate is for — we'll show you the numbers and the monthly picture side by side.

Flooring financing FAQ

Do you offer flooring financing?
Yes. Landmark Flooring offers financing so you can install your new floors now and pay over time in monthly payments. Financing is subject to credit approval through our financing partner; ask our team for the current plans and terms that apply to your project.
What can I include in financing?
Because we sell every floor and install it with the skilled installers on our team, financing can cover the whole project — your flooring materials, professional installation, and prep work like removal, haul-away and subfloor leveling — all in one monthly payment.
Is there a 0% or buy-now-pay-later option?
Promotional plans can vary, so we keep the details accurate to whatever our financing partner is offering at the time rather than promising a specific rate here. Just ask us when you request your estimate and we'll tell you exactly what's available.
How do I apply?
Start with a free estimate so you know the real project cost, then let us know you'd like to finance it. We'll guide you through a short application, and approval decisions are usually quick. Call (775) 297-3236 or request your free estimate to get started.
