## Keyword clusters → target page

### Local store / 'flooring near me' (brand-adjacent money cluster)  ·  _transactional · priority: critical_
- **Target page:** Homepage + a strong 'Flooring Store / Showroom' positioning; reinforced by GBP and Contact/Showroom page
- **Example queries:** flooring store near me, flooring stores near me, carpet stores near me, flooring store Carson City, flooring company Carson City NV, best flooring store near me, flooring showroom near me
- **Why:** These generic 'near me' / store queries are the single highest-demand, highest-intent flooring searches and trigger the Google Maps map pack, which competitors say drives 40-60% of flooring lead volume. As a physical Carson City showroom AND installer, Landmark must own this for its home market; the homepage and GBP are the primary assets here.

### Carpet (product + install)  ·  _commercial · priority: critical_
- **Target page:** Dedicated /carpet/ product page with an embedded or linked 'carpet installation' service section/page
- **Example queries:** carpet store near me, carpet installation near me, carpet installers near me, best carpet for living room, stain resistant carpet, carpet for bedrooms, carpet installation cost
- **Why:** Carpet is one of the highest-volume material categories and 'carpet stores near me' is a top head term. Landmark sells and installs carpet, so a dedicated page captures both product browsers and ready-to-install buyers instead of the current on-page anchor that ranks for nothing.

### Luxury Vinyl / LVP / LVT / waterproof  ·  _commercial · priority: critical_
- **Target page:** Dedicated /luxury-vinyl-flooring/ (LVP/LVT) product page + linked install/estimate CTA
- **Example queries:** luxury vinyl plank flooring, LVP flooring near me, waterproof vinyl flooring, vinyl plank flooring installation, luxury vinyl flooring, best LVP flooring brands, waterproof flooring for kitchens
- **Why:** LVP/waterproof vinyl is the fastest-growing, highest-search material in residential flooring and a core Landmark product. The 'waterproof' angle is a strong differentiator for kitchens/baths/rentals and pet/kid households, making this a top money page.

### Laminate  ·  _commercial · priority: high_
- **Target page:** Dedicated /laminate-flooring/ product page
- **Example queries:** laminate flooring near me, laminate flooring installation, best laminate flooring, laminate vs vinyl flooring, waterproof laminate flooring, laminate flooring cost
- **Why:** Laminate is a high-volume budget material that Landmark sells and installs; capturing it supports the 'budget options' differentiator and feeds comparison/cost informational queries (laminate vs vinyl) that pull top-of-funnel shoppers.

### Hardwood / wood (product, install, refinishing)  ·  _commercial · priority: high_
- **Target page:** Dedicated /hardwood-flooring/ product page (+ a /hardwood-floor-refinishing/ service page if refinishing is offered)
- **Example queries:** hardwood flooring near me, hardwood floor installation, hardwood floor refinishing near me, engineered hardwood flooring, hardwood flooring cost per square foot, oak vs maple flooring, wood floor repair
- **Why:** Hardwood carries the highest average job value ($6k-12k+ per sources) so even modest volume is lucrative. 'Hardwood floor refinishing' is a distinct high-intent service query; if Landmark offers it, it deserves its own page, otherwise the product page should still capture install demand.

### Custom rugs / area rugs  ·  _commercial · priority: medium_
- **Target page:** Dedicated /custom-rugs/ product page
- **Example queries:** custom area rugs near me, custom rugs Carson City, bound carpet rugs, custom rug binding near me, area rugs from carpet remnants
- **Why:** Custom rugs are a niche differentiator with low competition; a dedicated page lets Landmark own a long-tail category most flooring competitors ignore, and supports cross-sell to carpet buyers.

### Flooring installation (core service pillar)  ·  _transactional · priority: critical_
- **Target page:** Pillar /flooring-installation/ service page linking to per-material install sections/pages
- **Example queries:** flooring installation near me, flooring installation Carson City, flooring contractors near me, licensed flooring installer near me, professional flooring installation, flooring installation cost
- **Why:** Landmark's key differentiator is warrantied in-house, licensed/bonded/insured labor (the Landmark Guarantee). A pillar installation page captures buyers searching for an installer (not just a store) and anchors the cluster of per-material install queries. This is the highest-intent service money page.

### Estimates / quotes / measure  ·  _transactional · priority: high_
- **Target page:** Estimate/Contact conversion page (and prominent CTA sitewide)
- **Example queries:** free flooring estimate near me, free in-home flooring estimate, flooring quote Carson City, flooring measurement and estimate, in-home flooring consultation
- **Why:** 'Free estimate' is bottom-of-funnel, ready-to-convert intent and one of Landmark's explicit differentiators (free in-home & showroom estimates). A dedicated estimate page improves conversion and gives a landing target for ads.

### City / location landing pages  ·  _transactional · priority: critical_
- **Target page:** One dedicated city landing page per market (Reno, Sparks, Minden/Gardnerville, Lake Tahoe, Virginia City, Fallon; Carson City = home)
- **Example queries:** flooring Reno NV, carpet store Reno, flooring installation Reno, flooring Sparks NV, flooring Minden Gardnerville NV, flooring Lake Tahoe, flooring Fallon NV
- **Why:** Sources are unanimous: per-city pages are how a single-showroom business ranks across a multi-city service area. Reno/Sparks have far larger populations than Carson City, so dedicated city pages are essential to compete with Reno-based stores (Frankful, National Wholesale, Floor Coverings International) for those markets.

### Commercial flooring  ·  _commercial · priority: medium_
- **Target page:** Dedicated /commercial-flooring/ segment page
- **Example queries:** commercial flooring contractor near me, commercial flooring Reno, office flooring installation, commercial carpet tile, commercial LVT flooring, retail flooring installation
- **Why:** Commercial buyers research differently (durability, maintenance, project management) and are a named Landmark segment. A dedicated page lets Landmark speak to that audience and rank for distinct commercial queries that the residential pages won't.

### Property management / rental turns  ·  _commercial · priority: medium_
- **Target page:** Dedicated /property-management-flooring/ segment page
- **Example queries:** property management flooring contractor, apartment turn flooring near me, rental property flooring, fast flooring installation for rentals, durable LVP for rentals, bulk flooring for property managers
- **Why:** Property managers are a named Landmark segment with repeat, high-volume turn work and value speed/durability/warranty. This is low-competition B2B intent; a tailored page (pitching waterproof LVP + warrantied labor + fast turns) converts a lucrative recurring customer that generic pages miss.

### Cost / pricing guides  ·  _informational · priority: high_
- **Target page:** Resource hub / blog cost-guide articles linking to the relevant product and estimate pages
- **Example queries:** how much does flooring installation cost, carpet installation cost per square foot, LVP installation cost, hardwood flooring cost per square foot, cost to install laminate flooring, flooring cost calculator
- **Why:** Cost queries are extremely high-volume top-of-funnel research; sources call them 'good for lead capture.' Cost guides build topical authority, capture shoppers early, and funnel them to Landmark's free-estimate CTA before they reach a competitor.

### Comparison ('vs') guides  ·  _informational · priority: medium_

- **Example queries:** LVP vs laminate, laminate vs vinyl flooring, hardwood vs laminate, engineered vs solid hardwood, carpet vs hardwood for bedrooms, LVP vs tile
- **Why:** 'Vs' queries capture undecided buyers comparing materials; they're prime internal-linking hubs that route readers to the matching product money pages and demonstrate expertise (E-E-A-T).

### Best-flooring-for / use-case guides  ·  _informational · priority: medium_
- **Target page:** Resource hub / blog buying-guide articles linking to LVP/laminate/carpet product pages
- **Example queries:** best flooring for pets, best flooring for kids, best waterproof flooring for kitchens, best flooring for basements, best flooring for high traffic areas, best flooring for rentals, most durable flooring
- **Why:** Use-case 'best for' queries are high-volume informational searches that map directly to Landmark's waterproof LVP and durable options. They attract homeowners early, build authority, and pre-sell the product before the estimate.

### Trust / financing / about (supporting conversion)  ·  _commercial · priority: medium_
- **Target page:** Financing detail page, About page, and Reviews/Testimonials page
- **Example queries:** flooring financing near me, buy now pay later flooring, flooring 0% financing, licensed bonded insured flooring contractor, flooring warranty, flooring company reviews Carson City
